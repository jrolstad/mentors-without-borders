How to Prepare Biscuits

The simplest recipe

Ingredients

  • 80oz flour
  • 30oz sugar
  • margarine
  • milk
  • egg

Instructions

  1. Prepare a dough with the various ingredients
  2. knead and roll the prepared dough.
  3. cut into desired shapes and size
  4. Place on a greased baking tray
  5. Bake for about 20 minutes in an oven
  6. Serve with a cocktail
